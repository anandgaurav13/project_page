About BBAU

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Lucknow

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University

Vidya Vihar, Raebareli Road, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University (BBAU) is a Central University located in Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1996 as Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University by the Government of Uttar Pradesh, it was later upgraded to a Central University in 2009 under the Central Universities Act. The university is named after Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, the principal architect of the Indian Constitution and a champion of social justice and equality.

Mission

To provide quality education and foster research in various disciplines of knowledge, with special focus on promoting social justice, inclusivity, and empowerment of the marginalized sections of society.

The university strives to create a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, innovation, and holistic development of students to make them responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Vision

To emerge as a world-class center of excellence in higher education and research, committed to the ideals of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar - equality, social justice, and nation-building.

The university envisions creating a knowledge society that is inclusive, progressive, and contributes meaningfully to national and global development through cutting-edge research and quality education.
